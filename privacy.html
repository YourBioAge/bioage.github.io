Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Overview

BioAge ("the App") is an iOS application that estimates your biological age from health metrics and generates personalized longevity insights. Your privacy is a core design principle — all personal health data stays on your device.

Data Stored On-Device Only

The following data is stored locally in your device's Keychain and SwiftData database and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Personal profile (name, date of birth, biological sex, height, weight)
  • Health goals
  • Health snapshots (sleep, HRV, blood markers, cardiovascular metrics, stress levels)
  • Biological age scores and history

Data Sent Off-Device

When you request an AI insight, anonymous numeric scores (e.g., sleep score: 7.2, HRV score: 6.8) are sent to the Anthropic API to generate a personalized coaching insight. No personal identifiers, names, dates of birth, or raw health values are included in this request.

Apple Health Integration

The App requests read-only access to Apple Health data (sleep analysis, HRV, resting heart rate, blood oxygen). This data is processed on-device only and is never uploaded to any server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only external service used is the Anthropic API, which receives anonymous numeric scores as described above.

Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. Deleting the App removes all associated data. We have no copies of your data.

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support.bioage@gmail.com
