How BioAge calculates your score

Eight evidence-based pillars are scored independently and combined into a single biological age estimate. Missing pillars are gracefully excluded — the score always reflects the data you actually have.

🩸

Blood Markers

15 supported markers — glucose, HbA1c, CRP, cholesterol, and more — compared to evidence-based optimal ranges.

25%
😴

Sleep

Duration and quality scored using a 7–9h optimal window. Deep sleep and REM percentages contribute bonus points.

25%
💓

HRV

Your RMSSD compared to age- and sex-matched population norms (Nunan et al.). Higher HRV = biologically younger.

15%
🧘

Stress

Levels 1–4 are protective. Chronic high stress (5–10) adds years to your score. Self-reported and factored directly in.

bonus
🫀

Cardiovascular

Resting heart rate compared to age norms. Blood pressure optionally provides additional refinement.

12%
⚡

Reaction Time

Measured via the iPhone screen. Compared against Welford age-adjusted norms — a sensitive marker of neural aging.

9%
✊

Grip Strength

Peak force, stability, and duration — each weighted. Grip strength is one of the strongest predictors of all-cause mortality.

2%
🚶

Gait

Step cadence and regularity measured via the accelerometer. Optimal cadence is 100–130 steps per minute.

Weights are redistributed proportionally when pillars are absent. Confidence is reported as the fraction of pillars present — so you always know how complete your score is.
